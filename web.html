About Me

My name is Molly, I currently go to school at Tappan Zee High School where I am a junior. I began to get interested in programming as a freshman, when I started taking classes relating to computer science. In my sophomore year when I restarted in person school, I began to get very involved in my school's computer science program by taking all classes offered, joining all clubs, continuing traditions from past years, and started some new ones of my own. Since then, I have taken on many leadership positions relating to computer science. When I am not programming, I enjoy playing tennis,

Awards and Recognitions

Robotics

During the 2022 season, I assisted with programming different aspects of the robot including the serializer and the color sensor. I was the technician for these compeitions.
