About Me
My name is Molly Culot, I am sixteen years old and a junior at Tappan Zee High School. I started programming my freshman year, by taking entry level classes offered at my school. During that year I had all of my classes online, giving me the opportunity to focus on music and computer science in my free time. Going into my sophomore year I went back to in-person school, and I joined every computer science related extracurricular my school had to offer. Currently, in my junior year I took on many leadership roles in these activities, and started some new activities of my own. My favorite project I am managing is a program that helps students located in Sub-Saharan Africa access STEM related education. When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing the guitar, playing tennis, working at Starbucks, and organizing.
